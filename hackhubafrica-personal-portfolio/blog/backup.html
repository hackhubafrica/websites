Introduction

Cybersecurity(Security) is the practice of ensuring the C.I.A of infornation by protecting neworks ,devices ,people and data from unauthorized access or criminal exploitation. PII(Personal Identfiable Information) and SPII(Sensitive Personal Identfiable Information) are target by criminals for Identity Theft and Financial Gain.

The CIA Triad

    The CIA Triad is a foundatinal model that helps inform how organizations consider risk when setting up systems and security policies.
  1. 1.Confidentiality means only authorized users can access specific assets or data
  2. 2.Integrity means the data is correct ,authentic and reliable.
  3. 3.Availability means the data is accessible to those who are authorize to access it.

An asset is an item that is perceived as having value to an organization e.g an application that stores user's sensitive data such as account numbers ,credit cards
